🥗 Diet Tips

🥗 Balanced Macronutrients

  • Protein (20-30% of calories): Repairs muscles, supports growth
  • Sources: chicken, fish, eggs, paneer, lentils, beans, tofu.
  • Carbohydrates (40-50% of calories): Main energy source.
  • Sources: rice, oats, fruits, sweet potato, whole wheat bread.
  • Healthy Fats (20–30% of calories): Hormone balance & brain function.
  • Sources: nuts, seeds, olive oil, avocado, coconut, fish.

💧 Hydration

  • 2.5–3L water daily.
  • Add coconut water, lemon water, or buttermilk for electrolytes.
  • Drink more if you sweat a lot during workouts.

🕒 Meal Timing

  • Breakfast: High in protein + complex carbs for energy.
  • Pre-workout (1-2 hrs before): Carbs + protein (banana + peanut butter, oats + milk).
  • Post-workout (within 1 hr): Fast protein + carbs (whey + fruit, paneer + rice).
  • Dinner: Light, protein-rich, fewer carbs if not training late.

🥦 Micronutrients (Vitamins & Minerals)

  • Eat colorful fruits & vegetables daily (at least 3-4 servings).
  • Include green leafy veggies (iron, calcium).>
  • Fruits like oranges, berries, banana (for potassium, vitamin C).
  • Seeds & nuts for zinc, magnesium, vitamin E.

⛔ Foods to Limit

  • Junk food (fried, packaged snacks).
  • Sugary drinks (colas, energy drinks).
  • Excess refined carbs (white bread, pastries).
  • Too much salt & sugar.

🔥 Weight Loss Tips

  • Do regular cardio (running, cycling, HIIT).
  • Eat in a calorie deficit.
  • Cut down on sugary and processed foods.
  • 🍽️ Control portion sizes-eat until 80% full.
  • 🥗 Focus on high-protein foods (keeps you full, preserves muscle).
  • 🥦 Add more vegetables & fiber (low-calorie, high-volume).
  • 🍎 Choose whole foods over processed foods.
  • 🚫 Cut sugary drinks, sodas, and excess fruit juices.
  • 🥤 Drink 2.5-3L water daily (often thirst feels like hunger).
  • 🍳 Don't skip breakfast; include protein-rich options.
  • 🕒 Avoid late-night snacking.
  • 🍠 Prefer complex carbs (oats, brown rice, sweet potato) over refined carbs.
  • 🥑 Include healthy fats in moderation (nuts, seeds, olive oil).
  • 🍵 Limit fried foods, fast food, and packaged snacks.
  • 📉 Create a calorie deficit (burn more than you eat).
  • ⏱️ Eat at regular times; don't starve long hours.
  • 🛏️ Get enough sleep (poor sleep = weight gain).

💪 Weight Gain Tips

  • Eat in a calorie surplus (more calories than you burn).
  • Focus on protein-rich foods like chicken, eggs, fish.
  • Lift heavy weights to build muscle mass.
  • 🍽️ Eat in a calorie surplus (consume more than you burn).
  • 🥩 Increase protein intake (eggs, chicken, fish, paneer, legumes).
  • 🍚 Add more carbohydrates (rice, oats, whole wheat, potatoes).
  • 🥑 Include healthy fats (nuts, seeds, peanut butter, avocado, olive oil).
  • 🥛 Drink milk, smoothies, or shakes for extra calories.
  • 🍌 Choose calorie-dense snacks (nuts, dry fruits, granola bars).
  • 🥗 Don’t skip meals – eat 5–6 times a day.
  • 🕒 Focus on post-workout meals (protein + carb combo).
  • 🥤 Add healthy calorie boosters (honey, cheese, nut butter).
  • 🏋️ Pair with strength training to build lean muscle, not just fat.
  • 🛏️ Ensure 7–9 hrs of sleep for recovery & growth.

💪 Biceps Workout

  • 🥩 Protein for Muscle Repair & Growth
  • 🍚 Carbs for Energy
  • 🥑 Healthy Fats for Hormones
  • Whey protein-quick recovery after workouts.
  • Creatine monohydrate-boosts strength & muscle size.
  • BCAAs / EAAs-if training fasted or low on protein.
  • Fish oil-for joint health and recovery.

💪 Triceps Workout

  • Tricep Dips – 3 sets of 12 reps.
  • Overhead Tricep Extension – 3 sets of 10 reps.
  • Close-Grip Pushups – 4 sets of 8 reps.
  • 🍗 Eat high-protein meals to repair and grow triceps (chicken, eggs, paneer, lentils).
  • 🍚 Include complex carbs for workout energy (rice, oats, whole wheat, sweet potato).
  • 🥑 Add healthy fats in moderation (nuts, seeds, olive oil, avocado).
  • 🥤 Stay hydrated (2.5–3L water daily, electrolytes if sweating).
  • ⏱️ Have a pre-workout snack (carbs + protein, e.g., banana + peanut butter, oats + milk).
  • 💪 Take a post-workout meal (fast-digesting protein + carbs, e.g., whey + fruit, paneer + dates).
  • 🐟 Consider omega-3 sources (fish, flaxseeds) for recovery and joint health.
  • 🛏️ Ensure proper sleep & rest for muscle recovery.

💪 Abs Workout

  • Cut refined carbs & sugar (white rice, sweets, soft drinks).
  • Eat high-protein, moderate carbs, healthy fats.
  • More fiber (vegetables, salads, oats) → keeps stomach flat.
  • Avoid junk, oily, and alcohol → adds belly fat.
